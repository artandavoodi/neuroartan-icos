Layer boundary premise

The ICOS layer remains separate from the public website, the internal Office layer, and the future Investor layer. It should mature as the sovereign product platform for users, subscriptions, product systems, and application-facing infrastructure.

Platform compatibility track

ICOS should be structured from the start for modular interface systems, language awareness, theme compatibility, account-aware behavior, and future sovereign deployment without requiring architectural rebuild.

Product-facing information, subscriptions, model systems, and software layers should only move into ICOS through approved product, legal, and governance pathways. Public marketing and internal office operations remain structurally separate from this layer.
